===== FARMING SIMULATOR =====

Welcome to your farm!
You will plant crops, water them, and harvest them.

*** IMPORTANT GAME RULES ***
• A DAY ENDS AFTER 10 MOVES.
• Crops grow +1 stage per day.
• You may water ONE plant per day for +1 extra growth.

=== CONTROLS ===
Move: W A S D
Plant Carrot: C
Plant Lettuce: L
Plant Spinach: E
Plant Beet: B
Plant Brussel Sprouts: P
Harvest Mature Crops: H
Water a Plant: R
Quit Game: Q

Player character is: '@'

=== PLANT GROWTH ===
Seeds use unique tilled-soil symbols:
Carrot: '#' → 'c' → 'C'
Lettuce: '%' → 'l' → 'L'
Spinach: '*' → 'e' → 'E'
Beet: '&' → 'b' → 'B'
Brussel Sprouts: '?' → 'p' → 'P'

Days to sprout:
• Carrot = 1
• Lettuce = 2
• Spinach = 2
• Beet = 2
• Brussel Sprouts = 5

Days from sprout to mature:
• Carrot = 1
• Lettuce = 2
• Spinach = 3
• Beet = 5
• Brussel Sprouts = 10

=== WATERING ===
• You may water ONE plant per day.
• Watering instantly gives +1 growth.
• You must stand on the plant to water it.

=== FINAL NOTES ===
'.' = empty land
Manage your moves, watering, and planting to grow crops efficiently!
